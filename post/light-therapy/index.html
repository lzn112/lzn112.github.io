褪黑素发挥功能的时间，与 moring light 的关系

治疗应在预定起床时间后 10 分钟内开始。 在大多数情况下，治疗开始的时间会早于基线唤醒时间，而基线唤醒时间也与褪黑激素起效时间和 MEQ 分数高度相关，这取决于患者的习惯性睡眠时间。 例如，一个睡眠时间短的人，其就寝时间是午夜，早上 6 点醒来，他将在习惯性觉醒时开始治疗。 相反，如果睡眠时间较长，晚上 11:30 起床，早上 7:30 醒来，则必须提前 1 小时，即早上 6:30 醒来。
睡眠时间超过 6 小时后，每多睡半小时，光照治疗时的觉醒时间就会比基线时的习惯觉醒时间提前 15 分钟
如果睡眠时间延长到 9 小时，则最多提前 1.5 小时。 该算法应被视为确定最初光照时间的 "最佳猜测 "策略，可能需要根据早期结果进行调整。
MEQ 47 的在线版本（www.cet.org）会自动向用户返回建议的光照时间间隔。 虽然该算法基于 SAD 数据，但已成功应用于非季节性抑郁症患者48 和睡眠阶段延迟患者。
Sessions should begin within 10 minutes of scheduled wake-up time. In most cases, treatment will begin earlier than the baseline wake-up time—which is also highly correlated with melatonin onset and the MEQ score—depending on the patient’s habitual sleep duration. For example, a short sleeper, whose bedtime is at midnight and who awakens at 6 AM, would start treatment on habitual awakening. In contrast, a long sleeper, with onset at 11:30 PM and awakening at 7:30 AM, would have to wake up 1 hour earlier, at 6:30 AM. For every half hour of sleep beyond 6 hours, awakening for light treatment is 15 minutes earlier than habitual awakening at baselinea maximum of 1.5 hours earlier if sleep duration extends to 9 hours. The algorithm should be considered a “best guess” strategy to determine the initial timing of light exposure, with a potential need for adjustment depending on early results. An online version of the MEQ,47 at www.cet.org, automatically returns the recommended light exposure interval to the user. Although the algorithm is based on SAD data, it has been applied successfully to patients with nonseasonal depression48 and delayed sleep phase.
