Alpha Activity

Forms for Sleep Staging Alpha Activity (Alpha Rhytm)
8-13 hertz (Hz).

Most prominent over the occipital areas (alpha rhythm).

Activity increased by eye closure and attenuated by eye opening (alpha rhythm).

Characteristic electroencephalography (EEG) activity in drowsy, eyes-closed stage W
(alpha rhythm).

Common in rapid eye movement (REM) sleep (1-2 Hz slower than during stage W).

May occur with arousals (brief awakenings).

10% of persons do not produce alpha rhythm with eye closure.

阿尔法活动（阿尔法节律）
8-13 赫兹（Hz）。

枕骨区最为突出（α 节律）。

闭眼时活动增强，睁眼时活动减弱（α 节律）。

昏昏欲睡、闭眼阶段 W 的特征性脑电图（EEG）活动（α节律）。

常见于快速眼动（REM）睡眠（比 W 阶段慢 1-2 赫兹）。

可能在唤醒（短暂觉醒）时出现。

10%的人在闭眼时不会产生α节律。

11-16 Hz (classically 12-14 Hz).

Maximal over central areas.

Duration >0.5 sec (0.5-1.5 sec).

One of the defining characteristics of stage N2.

Thalamocortical oscillations (reticular thalamic nucleus).

May be seen in stage N3 sleep.

Drug spindles (benzodiazepines) may have a slightly faster frequency.

K-complex

High amplitude-biphasic deflection.
Awell-delineated negative sharp wave (upward) followed by a positive (downward) slow wave.
Stands out from the lower voltage background.
Duration >0.5 sec.
Characteristic of stage N2 sleep.
Maximal over frontal areas (frontal > central > occipital).
KC-associated arousal requires arousal to start no more than 1 second after KC termination.

高振幅双相偏转。
一个界限分明的负尖波（向上）紧接着一个正慢波（向下）。
从低电压背景中脱颖而出。
持续时间大于 0.5 秒。
N2 阶段睡眠的特征。
额叶区域最大（额叶 > 中央 > 枕叶）。
KC 相关唤醒要求在 KC 终止后 1 秒内开始唤醒。

眼电信号

Eye blinks

Conjugate vertical eye movements at a frequency of 0.5 to 2 hertz (Hz) present in wake- fulness with the eyes open or closed.

眨眼 ：睁眼或闭眼时出现的频率为 0.5 至 2 赫兹 (Hz) 的共轭垂直眼球运动。

Reading eye movements

Trains of conjugate eye movements consisting of a slow phase followed by a rapid phase in the opposite direction as the subject reads.

阅读眼动： 阅读眼球运动：当受试者阅读时，一连串的共轭眼球运动，包括一个慢速阶段和一个相反方向的快速阶段。

顾房间时）。

Slow eye movements

Conjugate, fairly regular, sinusoidal eye movements with an initial deflec- tion lasting >500 milliseconds (msec).

慢眼球运动： 共轭的、相当有规律的正弦眼球运动，初始变形持续时间大于 500 毫秒（msec）。

Rapid eye movements (REM)

Conjugate, irreg- ular, sharply peaked eye movements with an initial deflection usually lasting <500 msec. While rapid eye movements are characteristic of stage R sleep, they may also be seen in wakefulness with eyes open (as patients look around the room).

快速眼动（REM）： 共轭的、不规则的、尖锐的眼球运动，初始偏转通常持续 <500 毫秒。而 快速眼动是 R 睡眠阶段的特征，但在清醒状态下睁眼时也可能出现这种眼球运动（当患者环顾房间时）。

b. Other findings consistent with stage W (all dividuals)

Stage N3

N3-A Score stage N3 when 20% or more of an epoch consists of slow wave activity, irrespective of age. (20% of 30 second epoch=6 seconds)

EEG: SWA > 20% of the epoch (>6 seconds), sleep spindles may be present in stage N3.

EOG: Eye movements are not typically seen during stage N3 sleep.

EMG: In stage N3, chin EMG is of variable amplitude, often lower than in stage N2 sleep and sometimes as low as in stage R sleep.

N3-A 如果慢波活动占一个历时的 20%或更多，不论年龄如何，则记为 N3 阶段。(20% 30 秒的 20%=6 秒）
脑电图：慢波活动大于 20%（大于 6 秒），N3 阶段可能出现睡眠棘波。

眼电图：在 N3 阶段睡眠中通常不会出现眼球运动。

肌电图：在 N3 阶段，颏肌电图的振幅不一，通常低于 N2 睡眠阶段，有时甚至低于 R 睡眠阶段。 有时与 R 期睡眠时一样低。

Arousal

Score an arousal during sleep stages N1, N2, N3, or R if there is an abrupt shift of EEG frequency
including alpha, theta and/or frequencies greater than 16 Hz (but not spindles) that lasts at least 3 seconds, with at least 10 seconds of stable sleep preceding the change. Scoring of arousal during REM requires a concurrent increase in submental EMG lasting at least 1 second.

在睡眠阶段 N1、N2、N3 或 R，如果出现脑电图频率的突然变化，则将唤醒记为 "唤醒"。
包括α、θ和/或频率大于 16 赫兹（但不包括棘波），且持续至少 3 秒钟，在发生变化之前至少有 10 秒钟的稳定睡眠。快速动眼期唤醒的评分要求同时出现持续至少 1 秒的下额肌电图（submental EMG）增加。
